# Contributing to the Periodic Table of the Multiverse (PTM)

We welcome open collaboration from theoretical physicists, computational scientists, and mathematical researchers! 

## Contribution Guidelines
1. **Adding Elemental Triggers:** If you map a standard terrestrial element or isotope into a new dimension and calculate its mandatory anomaly-canceling mass via `ptm_generator.py`, please document your results in `EXAMPLES.md`.
2. **Optimizing the Filter:** Pull Requests focused on accelerating the trace computation of non-local heat-kernels or improving numerical performance in Python/NumPy are highly encouraged.
3. **Bug Reports:** If you discover a specific tensor mapping boundary where an infinity leaks past the regulator, please submit a report using our structured error form.
